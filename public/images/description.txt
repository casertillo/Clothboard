How it works
3 simple steps to create your daily fashion

Heading: Upload Your Items
upload your clothes from your device and categorise them into correct labels. They will be stored in your online cupboard.

Heading: Drag and Drop
Drag the folder from your online cupboard and then drop them onto the ClothBoard.

Heading: Create Your Fasion
Tab the arrow buttons to switch clothing to see different combinations. Enjoy creating your own style.
